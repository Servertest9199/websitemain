Shared Annotations

A bookworm's new collaborative way to annotate together.

Now What Is Shared Annotations?

Shared Annotations is a website that will allow readers to add and view their annotations on a shared pdf book with their reading buddy, so that they can see each others text and photo annotations while reading, instead of having to wait for book club. Users will do this by creating an account, then upload a pdf book to their library, share that copy, then enjoy reading and annotating together!

Who are we?

We are a group of university students who created a collaborative PDF annotation tool to enhance a reader's experience or help students study and share notes together.
